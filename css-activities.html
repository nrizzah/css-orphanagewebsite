ACTIVITIES IN RUMAH AMAN CHILDREN'S HOME

EDUCATION ACTIVITIES

Rumah Aman's main goal for the youngsters is quality education. There is a Malay proverb that goes, "Melentur buluh biarlah dari rebung," which translates as "Begin bending a bamboo from the shoot." Rumah Aman established an orphanage and welfare home for children aged three to six years old on this premise.

SECOND ACTIVITY

"The education programme of Rumah Aman is called I-SMART."

I-SMART : The teaching of Islam is integral in everything that we do, therefore the children will be instructed in its foundations to help them understand its importance and with that inculcates good habits, strengthens their good character, and personalities
